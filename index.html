Welcome to
The Polaris

Nestled in a peaceful residential lane, our boutique homestay is a sanctuary away from the hustle, yet perfectly positioned just a short stroll from Fort Kochi's rich colonial history.

Here, slow travel is celebrated. Whether you're enjoying a morning tea on a sunlit veranda or winding down after exploring the heritage streets, our space offers a warm, minimalistic retreat for the modern wanderer seeking deep rest.

Meet Tharun

Hi, I'm Tharun. Since 2026, I've had the absolute joy of welcoming travelers from around the world to Fort Kochi. My philosophy is simple: I don't just provide a room; I help you experience my home city like a local.

From mapping out hidden heritage walks and pointing you toward the best uncrowded cafes, to simply sharing a cup of freshly brewed South Indian coffee in the morning—I’m here (in English, Hindi, or Malayalam) to ensure your stay is authentic, effortless, and deeply memorable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you allow early check-ins or late check-outs? expand_more

We do our best to accommodate these requests based on availability. Please let us know your arrival time in advance.

Is food available at the homestay? expand_more

We don't run a full restaurant, but breakfast is included. You are also surrounded by spectacular cafes and seafood spots in Fort Kochi, all within a short 5-minute walk.

Can you help arrange local tours? expand_more

Absolutely! We gladly assist in booking reliable airport transfers, scenic backwater tours, and Kathakali show tickets through our trusted local partners.

Is parking available at the property? expand_more

Yes, we offer safe, complimentary parking for our guests on the premises. Just let us know you're driving so we can reserve a spot.

Is there Wi-Fi and is it suitable for remote work? expand_more

Yes! We provide high-speed, complimentary Wi-Fi across the entire property. Many guests love working from our shaded verandas or the quiet courtyard.
